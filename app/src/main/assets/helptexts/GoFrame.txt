Touch a piece of your color to be moved,
then touch destination square where move to.
Cursor is moved also by slide.
Piece or Square is selected when touched twice.
Yellow button under the board displaying position
is alternative of the 2nd touch
(OneTouch mode is optional on MainOption)
Captured pieces are listed under the board.
  Yellow boxes shows remaining diffrence count,
  White  boxes shows up to gameover count.
-----
Resign : Give-up and you lose this game.
  Push in your turn for Local match.
Close : This button appears after gameover.
  Start next game after once closed.
